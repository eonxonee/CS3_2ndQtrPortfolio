THOUGHTS:

First, let's go through the pros and cons of our schedule. We get a break that gives us time to go down to the Biology Classroom, a study period right before the "tedious" subjects (namely Music and Statistics), and a Valed class for the last period to wind down.

Personally, I would have preferred the Biology and Study Periods to be switched, as the sequence would be Biology, Music, then Lunch (all of which I go downstairs). However, I do understand that the Study Period is placed before the "tedious" subjects to help students focus better. Another improvement I think would be a longer break time (15 minutes).
